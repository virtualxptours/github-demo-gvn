Welcome to Martin Luther King Jr. Memorial Gardens!

Posted on January 1, 2023 by Angel Nieves
Raleigh, NC Raleigh Parks

Link to virtual tour!

This park is located off of Martin Luther King Jr. Blvd and Rock Quarry Road in Raleigh, NC. It is a part of the Raleigh parks system. Dedicated in April 4, 1997 and sculpted by North Carolina native Abbe Godwin. (DocSouth 2023)

The Statue stands 6 foot and is located in the center of a circular area next to a pathway leading to a civil rights fountain. Surrounding the statue are named floor bricks of supporters that contributed to creation of the facility.

The park has been used to celebrate the life and accomplishments of Dr. Martin Luther King and the many civil rights leaders that fought for change in a divided south.

Ammenities offered at the park

Located at the park for people to enjoy are 17 tables, one grill, and electricity available. Picnic shelter available for rent on the raleigh parks website.

360 photography was captured using the QooCam 8k Enterprise. Virtual tours are an excellent visual tool that can be used to market, educate, and fascinate.
